Privacy Policy for Weathernook App

Last updated: 08.29.2023

Welcome to Weather Nook! This Privacy Policy outlines how Weathernook ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when using our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to ensuring your privacy and the security of your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Usage:

Weathernook is designed to provide you with weather information and does not collect any personal or user data. We do not gather, store, or share any information that could be used to identify you.

Location Information:

Weather Nook may request access to your device's location to provide you with accurate weather forecasts based on your current location. However, we do not store or transmit this location data to our servers. Your location information is used solely within the app to display local weather information.

Third-Party Services:

Weathernook does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would require sharing your data with external parties.

Children's Privacy:

Weather Nook does not collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at weathernook@theengineer.dev.

Thank you for using Weathernook!
